Laura Silverstein

Laura Silverstein approached Elevated Web Systems to help her build a high converting personal brand website. She is a highly successful couples therapist and has a growing following on her Medium blog.

The goal of the site was to provide landing pages to send her Medium readers where we could collect email addresses to use for future marketing purposes. We accomplished this through the creation of lead magnets and inviting calls to action throughout the entire site.
