Tasty Table Market : Website Design

Tasty Table Market was hit hard by the COVID-19 pandemic. They were a successful corporate catering business serving meals by the hundred to large companies and office buildings.

Shortly after the event industry shut down, the owner converted some of his commercial kitchen space into a specialty market. His existing website www.tastytablecatering.com didn't fit the goals and clearly describe his new pivoted venture.

We designed and developed a single page landing page geared toward converting social media traffic. Our goal was simple, cleanly display the new services, unique menu, and daily specials.

In one week we had this new website live and being visited by members of the surrounding community. The targeting goal was people who lived within a ten-mile radius surrounding their Berwyn location.
