Our Process

Don't be intimidated by the steps we take to make the perfect website for you! You can choose to be as involved as you'd like or leave the majority up to our experience.

  • Research & Planning
  • Content Curation
  • Design
  • Development
  • Launch

Research & Planning

There is no guess work when it comes to designing and developing your new website.

During this phase of the process, we will have an extensive conversation about your goals, get a better understanding of your customers, and learn about your competition.

Content Curation

Strategic and well planned content designed to reach your target audience.

Every image and word on your new website should be done with thought and intent. Whether we are targeting specific keywords or compelling someone to act on a call to action, the content curation segment of our process implements and achieves goals set in the previous phase.

Design

Creating a digital representation of your brand

Fancy animations don't drive conversions. Content and user experience do. Here, we work through the user experience, brand implementation, and sales funnel for your potential customers. Engaging visuals and targeted messaging direct our design process.

Development

Responsive, mobile-friendly and optimized for speed.

Since we code our websites ourselves instead of using builders like Wordpress and Wix our sites are extremely well engineered from the beginning. We development websites with a mobile-first indexing approach so that they are optimized for SEO performance while still being beautiful, creative, and very functional.

Launch

Superior hosting and maintenance

Because we maintain our own servers instead of using a 3rd party provider we are able to provide the best in class perfomance, complimentary SSL certificates, and 99.9% uptime. Offering affordable monthly maintence plans that cover site security updates, minor copy or image changes, and scheduled form testing.
